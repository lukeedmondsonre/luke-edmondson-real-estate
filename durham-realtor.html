About Durham

Why People Choose Durham, CA

Durham sits in the heart of Butte County's agricultural belt, surrounded by orchards, rice fields, and farmland that has defined the area for generations. It's the kind of town where people know their neighbors, the pace slows down, and the connection to the land feels real. For buyers tired of urban density and suburban sprawl, Durham offers something increasingly hard to find — genuine rural California living within easy reach of Chico's amenities.

Homes in Durham tend to offer more space per dollar than comparable properties in Chico, and the community's proximity to Highway 99 keeps commuting manageable for those who need access to the city. Whether you're looking for a family home with a large lot, a property with agricultural potential, or a quiet place to put down roots away from the noise, Durham delivers.

Buying a Home in Durham

Durham's real estate market is relatively small, which means inventory can be limited and good properties move when they're priced right. Luke helps buyers navigate the local market — evaluating lot sizes, agricultural considerations, and the rural property factors that don't come into play in the city.

Selling a Home in Durham

Selling in Durham means reaching buyers who understand and value the rural lifestyle. Luke's marketing approach targets the right audience — people looking for space, quiet, and agricultural character — and his pricing expertise ensures you're not leaving money on the table in a market where comparable sales can be sparse.
