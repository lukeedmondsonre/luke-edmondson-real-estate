About Orland

Why People Love Living in Orland

Orland is the kind of place where neighbors wave, the pace is unhurried, and there's room to breathe. Anchored by agriculture — olives, almonds, and dairy — and the recreational Black Butte Lake just to the west, Orland combines authentic rural living with the convenience of an I-5 location. Its historic downtown and strong community make it a favorite for families putting down roots.

For homebuyers, Orland is all about space and value. Larger lots, properties with acreage, and room for animals, gardens, or workshops are far more attainable here than in larger cities. Whether you're after a comfortable family home in town or a country property with land, Orland delivers more for your money than almost anywhere in the region.
