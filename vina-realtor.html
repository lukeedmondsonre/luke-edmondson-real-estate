About Vina

Why People Choose Vina, CA

Vina is home to one of California's oldest landmarks, the New Clairvaux Abbey, a Cistercian monastery with vineyards and a history stretching back to the land originally owned by Leland Stanford. The surrounding area is defined by agriculture, the Sacramento River, and an unhurried pace of life that's increasingly rare anywhere in California.

For buyers, Vina represents an opportunity for genuine rural living at prices that reflect the area's limited profile. Properties here tend toward larger parcels and agricultural character, ideal for buyers seeking space, privacy, and a connection to the land. It's a small market, but for the right buyer, it offers something truly distinctive.
