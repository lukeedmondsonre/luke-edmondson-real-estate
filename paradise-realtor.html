About Paradise

Why People Are Choosing Paradise

Perched in the pine-covered foothills above the valley, Paradise offers cooler temperatures, fresh mountain air, and a tight-knit community spirit that's nothing short of inspiring. Since 2018, the town has been on a remarkable journey of renewal — and today it represents one of the most unique opportunities in Northern California real estate, with brand-new, modern homes built to the latest safety standards.

For buyers, Paradise offers something rare: the chance to own a newly built home on an established lot, surrounded by nature, at prices well below comparable new construction elsewhere in the state. The community's resilience and momentum have made it attractive to families, retirees, and anyone seeking value, space, and a genuine sense of belonging.
