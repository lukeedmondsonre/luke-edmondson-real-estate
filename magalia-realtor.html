About Magalia

Why People Choose Magalia, CA

Magalia is the northern extension of the Paradise Ridge community, perched above the valley floor in the pines and cedars of the Butte County foothills. Cooler summers, fresh mountain air, and a genuinely tight-knit community define life here. Like neighboring Paradise, Magalia has its own story of resilience and renewal, and the community spirit that runs through both towns is unmistakable.

For buyers, Magalia offers foothill living at prices well below comparable mountain communities in other parts of California. The combination of natural setting, community character, and relative affordability makes it an attractive option for those who want space, nature, and a sense of belonging without the premium price tag.

Buying a Home in Magalia

Buying on the ridge — whether in Magalia or Paradise — comes with unique considerations: fire insurance and septic systems are two of the most important factors depending on the property. Luke navigates these with buyers and makes sure you understand exactly what you're taking on.

Selling a Home in Magalia

Selling in Magalia requires reaching buyers who appreciate the ridge lifestyle and understand its unique characteristics. Luke combines professional marketing with genuine local knowledge to connect your property with motivated, informed buyers.
