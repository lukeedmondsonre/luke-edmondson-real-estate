Luke's Listing Process

  • 1

    Free Comparative Market Analysis (CMA)

    Luke researches your neighborhood's recent sales, active competition, and market trends to price your home strategically, not too high (which leads to stale listings) and not too low (which leaves money on the table).

  • 2

    Pre-Listing Preparation Consultation

    Luke walks through your home and identifies the highest-ROI improvements: what to fix, what to stage, and what to skip.

  • 3

    Professional Photography & Marketing Materials

    Professional photography is one of the most powerful tools in real estate marketing, great photos capture attention online, create an emotional connection, and drive buyers to schedule showings.

  • 4

    Maximum Exposure Launch

    Your home hits the MLS and syndicates to thousands of websites across the internet, Zillow, Realtor.com, Homes.com, Redfin, Trulia, and many more, putting it in front of qualified buyers immediately.

  • 5

    Strategic Offer Management

    Luke reviews every offer in detail, advises you on the full picture, not just price, and negotiates the best possible outcome, whether it's one offer or multiple competing bids.

  • 6

    Smooth Escrow & Close

    Luke, and his assistant at times, manages the entire escrow process, coordinating inspections, appraisals, buyer financing, and title so you can focus on your next chapter without stress.
