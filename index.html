Meet Luke Edmondson

Luke Edmondson has spent the last seven years building a real estate practice rooted in one thing: doing right by the people he works with. Based in Chico and active across seventeen Northern California communities, he's helped hundreds of families buy, sell, and move forward — and a significant share of his business comes from clients referring him to people they care about.

Last year, Luke closed 77 sides — a number that reflects not just volume, but consistency, trust, and a relentless work ethic. He was recognized as the top individual agent in the Keller Williams Northern California & Hawaii region. But the number Luke is most proud of is his 71 five-star reviews, because those come from people whose names and stories he knows.
