Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making this website accessible to everyone.

Effective Date: July 12, 2026  |  Operator: Luke Edmondson Real Estate — Keller Williams Realty  |  DRE#: 02081864

Our Commitment

Luke Edmondson Real Estate is committed to ensuring that lukeedmondsonrealestate.com is accessible to people with disabilities. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to real estate information and services, and we take our responsibility under the California Unruh Civil Rights Act (Civil Code § 51) and the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) seriously.

We are actively working to improve the accessibility of this Site to achieve conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, the recognized standard for digital accessibility.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented or are working to implement the following accessibility features on this Site:

  • Semantic HTML structure — Pages use proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3) to help screen reader users navigate content efficiently.
  • Alt text for images — Meaningful images include descriptive alt text so screen reader users understand the visual content. Decorative images are marked appropriately.
  • Keyboard navigation — The Site is designed to be navigable using a keyboard alone, without requiring a mouse.
  • Sufficient color contrast — We aim for a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for body text and 3:1 for large text, consistent with WCAG 2.1 AA requirements.
  • Descriptive link text — Links are written to make sense out of context (e.g., "Contact Luke" rather than "click here").
  • ARIA labels — Interactive elements such as the mobile navigation button include ARIA labels for assistive technologies.
  • Responsive design — The Site adapts to different screen sizes, including mobile devices and tablets, without loss of content or functionality.
  • Focus indicators — Keyboard focus is visible on interactive elements so keyboard-only users can track their location on the page.
  • Language declaration — The HTML lang attribute is set to "en" so screen readers apply the correct language profile.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we recognize that some areas of this Site may not yet fully conform to WCAG 2.1 AA. Known areas of ongoing improvement include:

  • Third-party embed content — The MLS/IDX property listing embed on the Listings page and the Google Maps embed on the Contact page are served by external providers (CRMLS and Google, respectively). We do not control their accessibility and cannot guarantee they meet WCAG standards.
  • PDF documents — The Buyer's Guide PDF available for download may not be fully accessible. If you need this content in an accessible format, please contact us and we will provide the information in an alternative format.
  • Animation effects — Some page elements use scroll-triggered fade-in animations. While these respect the user's system-level reduced-motion preference when possible, some animations may still appear on certain devices.

We are committed to remediating known issues as our website development continues.

Technical Standards

This Site aims to conform to the following standards:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA — Web Content Accessibility Guidelines, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C)
  • California Unruh Civil Rights Act — Civil Code § 51, which prohibits discrimination in business establishments including digital commerce
  • ADA Title III — Requirements for public accommodations, which courts have increasingly applied to commercial websites

Feedback and Assistance

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this Site. If you experience any barriers, find content that is inaccessible, or need information in an alternative format, please contact Luke directly. We take accessibility feedback seriously and will respond as quickly as possible.

When contacting us about an accessibility issue, please describe the barrier you encountered, the page or feature involved, and the assistive technology you are using (if any). This helps us address the issue as effectively as possible.

If you are unable to access any content or use any feature on this Site due to a disability, we will make every reasonable effort to provide that content or service to you through an alternative means — including by phone, email, or in person.

Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility feedback, you may also file a complaint with the California Department of Fair Employment and Housing (DFEH) or the U.S. Department of Justice regarding ADA compliance. We strongly encourage you to contact us first so we can work to resolve any issue directly.
