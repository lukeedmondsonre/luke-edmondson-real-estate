Every Community

The Complete Service Area Guide

A closer look at all 17 communities Luke proudly serves across Northern California.

🌳

Durham, CA Butte County

Just south of Chico, Durham is a tight-knit rural community prized for its top-rated schools, larger parcels, and agricultural heritage. It's an ideal spot for buyers who want a country feel with quick access to Chico's amenities. Luke regularly helps families find ranch-style homes and properties with acreage here.

🌲

Magalia, CA Butte County

Nestled in the pines above Paradise, Magalia offers forested lots, cooler temperatures, and some of the most affordable foothill living in the region. With ongoing rebuilding and new construction, it's an area full of opportunity for buyers seeking value and natural beauty.

🌾

Gridley, CA Butte County

A classic Sacramento Valley farm town, Gridley combines small-town affordability with a strong sense of community. Known for its orchards and the annual Red Suspenders Day, Gridley appeals to buyers looking for character homes and excellent value south of Oroville.

🚜

Biggs, CA Butte County

One of the smallest incorporated cities in the area, Biggs is quiet, agricultural, and genuinely neighborly. Its affordability and rural charm make it a hidden gem for buyers who want to escape the bustle while staying close to Gridley and Oroville.

🦆

Willows, CA Glenn County

The seat of Glenn County, Willows sits along I-5 and is surrounded by rice fields and the Sacramento National Wildlife Refuge. It offers very affordable home prices, a friendly downtown, and easy valley access — a great choice for first-time buyers and investors alike.

🫒

Corning, CA Tehama County

Known as the "Olive City," Corning is a welcoming valley town with affordable housing, agricultural surroundings, and a relaxed pace of life. Its convenient I-5 location makes it popular with commuters and those seeking strong value in Tehama County.

🐎

Cottonwood, CA Tehama / Shasta County

Straddling the Tehama–Shasta county line, Cottonwood is horse country — known for its rodeo, larger acreage properties, and rural lifestyle. Buyers seeking land, equestrian facilities, or a country home with space find excellent options here.

🌉

Red Bluff, CA Tehama County

The Tehama County seat, Red Bluff sits on the Sacramento River with historic Victorian homes, a walkable downtown, and a strong sense of history. It offers a range of housing from charming older homes to newer developments, all at compelling valley prices.

🍇

Vina, CA Tehama County

A small agricultural community along Deer Creek, Vina is known for its vineyards and the historic Abbey of New Clairvaux. Rural, peaceful, and rich in land opportunities, it's perfect for buyers who value privacy and open space.

🌅

Los Molinos, CA Tehama County

Sitting along Highway 99 and the Sacramento River, Los Molinos is a friendly farming town offering riverfront properties, agricultural land, and affordable homes. It's a wonderful fit for buyers who want a slower pace and a connection to the land.

🌻

Gerber, CA Tehama County

A quiet unincorporated community south of Red Bluff, Gerber offers some of the most affordable rural living in the region. With agricultural surroundings and generous lot sizes, it appeals to buyers looking to stretch their budget and enjoy country life.

🏞️

Redding, CA Shasta County

The largest city in the North State, Redding is a regional hub for shopping, healthcare, and outdoor adventure — home to the iconic Sundial Bridge and gateway to lakes, rivers, and Mount Shasta. With diverse neighborhoods and a wide price range, Redding offers something for every buyer.

🛶

Anderson, CA Shasta County

Just south of Redding, Anderson blends affordability with convenience. Home to Anderson River Park and easy I-5 access, it's a popular choice for families and commuters who want value without sacrificing amenities. Luke helps buyers and sellers navigate this growing market with confidence.
