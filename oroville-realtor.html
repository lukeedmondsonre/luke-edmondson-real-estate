About Oroville

Why People Love Living in Oroville

Oroville is one of Northern California's best-kept secrets. Wrapped around Lake Oroville and the Feather River, it offers boating, fishing, hiking, and some of the most beautiful spring wildflower displays in the state at Table Mountain. The historic downtown — with its preserved Chinese Temple and classic brick storefronts — gives the city a sense of history you won't find in newer suburbs.

For homebuyers, Oroville delivers exceptional value. Your dollar stretches significantly further here than in many neighboring areas, making it a favorite for first-time buyers, retirees, investors, and anyone who wants more home — or more land — for the money. From lakeview properties on Kelly Ridge to affordable family homes near the heart of town, the variety is remarkable.
