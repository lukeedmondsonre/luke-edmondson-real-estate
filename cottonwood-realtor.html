About Cottonwood

Why People Choose Cottonwood, CA

Cottonwood is the kind of community that rewards buyers looking for space and quiet without sacrificing access to a larger city. Just south of Redding along the I-5 corridor, it offers the rural character of Tehama and Shasta counties with reasonable proximity to Redding's services and employment base.

Properties in Cottonwood often include larger lots, acreage, and the kind of space that simply isn't available in more densely populated areas. For buyers who want room to grow a garden, keep animals, run a home business, or simply have elbow room — Cottonwood delivers that at a price point that makes sense.
