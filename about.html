The Journey

Luke's Story in Real Estate

2019

Getting Started

Luke earned his real estate license and joined Keller Williams Realty in the Chico area, driven by his passion for entrepreneurship and a genuine desire to serve people well. He dove in with energy, building relationships and learning the market from the ground up.

2020–2021

Building by Referral

As satisfied clients began referring friends and family, Luke's business grew organically. He expanded his work to Oroville, Orland, Durham, and the surrounding valley communities, always led by where he could genuinely help people.

2021–2022

Paradise & the Foothills

When Paradise began rebuilding after the Camp Fire, Luke was there, helping families navigate the process of buying and selling in one of the most unique real estate markets in the region.

2023

Growing Across the North State

Luke continued expanding his reach into Tehama and Shasta Counties, serving clients from Red Bluff and Corning to Redding and Anderson. The North State became his territory, and he built the local knowledge to match.

2025

77 Sides Closed in 2025

Luke closed 77 transactions in 2025, a milestone that reflects years of relationship-building, market expertise, and a relentless commitment to the people he works with. He was recognized as a top individual agent in the Keller Williams Northern California & Hawaii region.

Today

Still Showing Up

With hundreds of transactions closed and 71 five-star reviews, Luke continues to show up for clients the same way he always has, with honesty, care, and a genuine desire to help people make one of the biggest decisions of their lives with confidence.
