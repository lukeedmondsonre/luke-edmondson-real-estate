About Chico

Why People Love Living in Chico

Chico is the heart of the North State — a thriving college town built around the tree-lined campus of California State University, Chico and the lush, 3,600-acre Bidwell Park, one of the largest municipal parks in the country. It's a place where farmers' markets, a walkable downtown, craft breweries, and a genuine sense of community come together to create one of the most desirable lifestyles in inland California.

For homebuyers, Chico offers remarkable variety. You'll find stately historic homes with character in The Avenues, family-friendly subdivisions in the northwest, upscale living with views in Canyon Oaks and Foothill Park, and everything in between. Compared to much of California, Chico remains relatively affordable — which is exactly why it continues to draw families, remote workers, retirees, and investors from across the state.
